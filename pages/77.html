The 1st Coq Workshop

Munich, Germany
21 August, 2009
(a satellite workshop of TPHOLs 2009)

The Coq workshop will bring together Coq users, developers and contributors. The workshop will be organized from submitted papers, invited talks and a plenary discussion on the evolution and design of Coq (call for papers).
The workshop proceedings will be post-published as a special issue of the Journal of Formalized Reasoning.
The workshop is supported by the Coq Technological Development Action at INRIA
