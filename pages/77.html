The 1st Coq Workshop

Munich, Germany
21 August, 2009
(a satellite workshop of TPHOLs 2009)

The Coq workshop will bring together Coq users, developers and contributors. It will be organized from accepted papers, invited talks and a plenary discussion on the evolution and design of Coq (see the call for papers). Journal versions of the workshop papers will be considered for publication as a special issue of the Journal of Formalized Reasoning.

Information for authors: Final camera-ready version expected on July 15, 2009.

There will be an invited talk shared with PLMMS.

The workshop is supported by the Coq Technological Development Action at INRIA.
