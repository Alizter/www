Documentation

Online documentation:

  • The Tutorial is a commented interactive session which introduces basics on terms, proofs, and tactics, written by Gérard Huet, Gilles Kahn and Christine Paulin-Mohring.
  • The Reference Manual is the complete, authoritative source of documentation for Coq.
  • You can also have a look to the Frequently Asked Questions...
  • ... and to the less frequently asked ones in Coq-Club mailing-list archives.

Additional documentation:

  • Cocorico!, the Coq wiki
  • The Coq'Art, the first (and only) book dedicated to the Coq proof assistant (Yves Bertot, Pierre Casteran, May 2004)
  • Coq in a Hurry (Yves Bertot, May 2006)
  • A Tutorial on Recursive Types in Coq (Eduardo Giménez, Pierre Castéran, Aug 2006) and the associated examples.
