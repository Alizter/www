Most of the previous version of Coq are available here for the most recent ones and here for the older ones.

Extensions of Coq

We cordially invite users to test and evaluate the small scale reflection package (ssreflect). It contains a robust and compact tactic language and basic libraries that served, among other applications, as support for the full formalization of the 4 color theorem in Coq.
