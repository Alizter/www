A Coq working group took place on february 14th 2013 at Paris 7 University. About 20 persons participated and it consisted of the following presentations: Enrico Tassi: Adding a positive/negative cache to the term comparison routine. Maxime Dénès: A native compiler for Coq: current status, perspectives and discussion François Ripault: Introducing the new coqdoc Micaela Mayero: Experimentations sur les réels de Coq. Pierre-Nicolas Tollitte: Extraction de code à partir de spécifications inductives.
