The Coq Workshop series brings together Coq users, developers and contributors. It is a one-day event, held virtually every year since 2009, and generally affiliated with ITP.

While conferences usually provide a venue for traditional research papers, the Coq Workshop focuses on strengthening the Coq community and providing a forum for discussing practical issues, including the future of the Coq software and its associated ecosystem of libraries and tools.

  • 2019, September 8th, Portland, OR, USA
  • 2018, July 8th, Oxford, UK
  • 2016, August 26th, Nancy, France
  • 2015, June 26th, Sophia Antipolis, France
  • 2014, July 18th, Vienna, Austria
  • 2013, July 22nd, Rennes, France
  • 2012, August, Princeton, NJ, USA
  • 2011, August 26th, Nijmegen, Netherlands
  • 2010, July 9th, Edinburgh, Scotland
  • 2009, August 21st, Munich, Germany

The workshop is supported by Inria.
