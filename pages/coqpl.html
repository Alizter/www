The CoqPL Workshop brings together Coq users, developers and contributors. It is a one-day event, held every year since 2015, at POPL.

  • 2020, January 25th, New Orleans, Louisiana, USA
  • 2019, January 19th, Lisbon Portugal
  • 2018, January 13th, Los Angeles, CA, USA
  • 2017, January 21st, Paris, France
  • 2016, January 23rd, St. Petersburg, FA, USA
  • 2015, January 18th, Mumbai, India

The workshop is supported by Inria.
